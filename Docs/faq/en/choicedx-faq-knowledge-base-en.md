## 3. AI Analysis, Accuracy & Use Cases

### Q1. What is AI skin analysis?

AI skin analysis is a technology that analyzes skin condition based on imaging or measurement data and provides information for consultation. ChoiceDx helps beauty retailers and brands build AI-powered skin analysis experiences.

### Q2. How does AI skin diagnosis work?

AI skin diagnosis uses image and measurement data to analyze skin condition and organize results for customer consultation. Depending on the product setup, it can also support scalp and hair analysis.

### Q3. How accurate is AI skin analysis?

Accuracy can vary depending on lighting, imaging conditions, measurement methods, product configuration, and store operation. ChoiceDx focuses on delivering consistent measurement experiences and AI-powered analysis results for consultation and product recommendation.

### Q4. How accurate is the analysis?

Accuracy is grounded in AI models trained on large-scale diagnostic data and a standardized optical/imaging setup, delivering consistent, reproducible results across repeated measurements. It analyzes over 20 scientific parameters for skin, scalp, and hair.

### Q5. Can it analyze a wide range of ethnicities and skin types?

Yes. ChoiceDx is trained on millions of diagnostic data points and analyzes by ethnicity and skin type, so it delivers reliable results across a wide range of ethnicities and skin types.

### Q6. Is AI skin analysis safe?

ChoiceDx
is designed to provide a non-invasive skin, scalp, and hair analysis experience. Safe operation should follow each store’s privacy policy, customer guidance process, and local operating rules.

### Q7. What is the difference between a skin diagnostic device and a skin analyzer?

A skin diagnostic device generally refers to a device or system that measures skin condition and provides consultation results. A skin analyzer focuses on analyzing imaging or measurement data. ChoiceDx can support both roles as a skin diagnostic device, skin analyzer, and skin measurement system.

### Q8. What does a skin measurement device measure?

The measurement items may vary by product and service configuration. ChoiceDx can provide key information related to skin, scalp, and hair conditions for consultation and personalized product recommendation.

### Q9. How are skin analysis results used?

Skin analysis results can be used for in-store consultation, customer education, personalized product recommendations, repeat visits, and branded retail experiences.

### Q10. What does AI scalp analysis check?

AI scalp analysis helps organize scalp and hair condition information for consultation. Depending on the product configuration, it can be used with scalp diagnostic devices, hair analysis devices, and mobile consultation environments.

### Q11. Can a scalp diagnostic device support hair loss consultation?

A scalp diagnostic device can help visualize scalp and hair conditions for consultation. However, medical diagnosis or treatment decisions should be made by healthcare professionals. ChoiceDx supports retail consultation and product recommendation experiences.

### Q12. Do you also provide product recommendation and care-related services?

We provide skin/scalp analysis and product recommendation features, which can be utilized by the professional performing the diagnosis.

### Q13. Does ChoiceDx support product recommendations?

ChoiceDx
