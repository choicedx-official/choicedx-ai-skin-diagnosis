## Why AI Skin Diagnosis Matters In Offline Beauty Retail

Offline beauty consultation starts with the customer's concerns, but customers often find it difficult to explain their skin condition precisely.

Concerns such as dryness, oiliness, pores, sensitivity, skin tone, and elasticity are easy to feel but difficult to measure objectively.

AI skin diagnosis provides a shared reference point between customer and consultant. With ChoiceDx Skin Scan, customers can understand their skin condition through data, while consultants can provide more consistent, specific, and personalized product recommendations.
