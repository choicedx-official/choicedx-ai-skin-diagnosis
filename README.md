# ChoiceDx Skin Scan

ChoiceDx Skin Scan is an AI-based skin diagnosis solution developed and operated by ChoiceTech Korea. It helps offline beauty retail stores analyze customer skin conditions and connect diagnosis results to consultation and personalized product recommendations.

## What is ChoiceDx?

ChoiceDx is an AI skin, scalp, and hair diagnosis solution by ChoiceTech Korea. It supports Skin Scan experiences, beauty retail consultation, and personalized product recommendation in offline retail environments.
