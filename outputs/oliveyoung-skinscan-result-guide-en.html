What Can Olive Young Skin Scan Show?

Olive Young Skin Scan captures skin or scalp images and helps customers understand their current status through image-based analysis.

Skin diagnosis can help review surface-related conditions, while scalp diagnosis can help customers look at areas that are difficult to check visually.

Available analysis items may vary depending on the store and operating environment. Always follow the actual items shown on the screen.

05

How Should You Read Olive Young Skin Scan Results?

Do not judge the results by one number alone. Read the explanatory text and priority care items shown on the result screen together.

Start by checking which items need relatively more attention. If colors, grades, and descriptions are provided, read the written explanation as well.

Skin type is a relatively stable characteristic, while skin condition can change with season, sleep, environment, and lifestyle. Repeated measurements under similar conditions can be more useful than a single result.

06

How Can Results Help Product Selection?

Skin Scan results should not be treated as an absolute purchasing rule. They are a reference for understanding current concerns and deciding which care direction to explore first.

A helpful flow is to check the result, identify priority concerns, explore product categories, compare ingredients and texture, consult staff, and review the routine again later.

Showing the result screen during store consultation can help staff understand the customer's concern faster and explain options more clearly.

Tips For More Stable Measurement

Follow the screen guide and align the face or scalp position as instructed.

Avoid covering the measurement area with glasses, bangs, or hands, and minimize movement during capture.

Results can vary with daily skin and scalp condition and environment. Use them as reference information for status review, consultation, and product discovery, not as medical diagnosis.
