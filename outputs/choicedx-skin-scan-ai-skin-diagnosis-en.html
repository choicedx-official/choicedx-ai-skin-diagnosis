Why AI Skin Diagnosis Matters In Beauty Retail

In-store beauty consultation usually starts with customer concerns.

Customers may say that their skin feels dry, oily, dull, sensitive, or that their pores are noticeable. These concerns are real, but they are often difficult to explain objectively. Without a shared reference point, it can be hard for both the customer and the consultant to understand the current skin condition and decide which products or care direction may be relevant.

Beauty consultants also have to understand each customer quickly. Every customer describes their skin differently, and consultation time is often limited. AI skin diagnosis provides a common reference that both the customer and consultant can review together.

ChoiceDx Skin Scan analyzes the customer's skin condition and helps translate diagnostic results into consultation language, product recommendation, and skincare guidance.
