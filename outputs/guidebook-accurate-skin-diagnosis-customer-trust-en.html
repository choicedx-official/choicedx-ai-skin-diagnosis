Customer Trust Starts With Explainable Diagnosis

In beauty retail, customers often describe their skin through feelings such as dryness, oiliness, dullness, sensitivity, or visible pores. These concerns are real, but they are not always easy to translate into a care direction.

Accurate skin diagnosis gives customers and consultants a shared reference point. A ChoiceDx Skin Scan experience can turn subjective concerns into visual information and consultation language.

Trust grows when customers understand the reason behind a recommendation. When the diagnosis, concern, and product suggestion are connected, consultation feels like a problem-solving process rather than a sales pitch.

02

What The Device Does And What The Consultant Does

A skin diagnosis device is the starting point. It captures images or measurement data and helps observe visible skin condition more consistently.

The consultant still plays the central role. The consultant explains how indicators such as moisture, oiliness, pores, tone, and elasticity relate to the customer’s actual concern.

A strong in-store diagnosis experience connects device, AI analysis, report, consultant interpretation, and product recommendation into one flow.

03

Why Accurate Diagnosis Makes Recommendations More Persuasive

Customers want to know why a specific product is being recommended. A recommendation based on their skin condition feels more relevant than a generic promotion.

When AI skin analysis results are organized into a consultation report, staff can explain product suggestions more clearly and reduce uncertainty during the purchase decision.

Accurate diagnosis also matters after purchase. Revisit comparison helps customers understand how their routine is developing over time.

04

A Retail Workflow That Builds Trust

The workflow is simple: listen to the concern, scan the skin, interpret the AI-assisted result, connect the result to product groups, and save the record for future comparison.

This reduces dependence on individual staff experience and helps stores provide a more consistent consultation quality.

In retail, the value is not complexity. The value is whether the customer can understand the result and trust the next recommendation.

Skin Scan At Olive Young Busan Jeonpo Station In Seomyeon

At Olive Young Busan Jeonpo Station in Seomyeon, customers use ChoiceDx at side-by-side Skin Scan booths to receive AI-assisted skin analysis in the store.

In the middle of Seomyeon’s youth shopping district, the round glass-fronted Olive Young store is marked by a clear OLIVE YOUNG sign. After entering through the door with the message “WELCOME WE ARE OLIVE YOUNG,” customers step into a two-floor retail space.

The Skin Scan zone inside the store looks different from the silver booths seen in earlier store environments. Two newly designed booths with gold-tone arch frames and orange curtains are placed next to each other, with a concierge desk nearby.

On the booth screen, customers see the message asking whether they need help with skin diagnosis, along with language buttons for Korean, English, Chinese, and Japanese. Once the screen is touched, ChoiceDx begins guiding the customer through the diagnosis experience.

Because two booths can operate at the same time, the space captures different customer scenes at once: young friends looking at the screens side by side, and customers with children waiting for diagnosis. This reflects Seomyeon’s mixed retail traffic, from young shoppers to family visitors.

After measurement, consultation-support results based on diagnostic data are saved in the Olive Young app. Customers often move naturally toward the skincare area after a short conversation.

FAQ

Why does accurate skin diagnosis matter for customer trust?

Because it helps customers understand the reason behind a recommendation and makes consultation more explainable.

Does a skin diagnosis device replace consultants?

No. The device provides data, while consultants connect the result to customer concerns and product recommendations.

Is ChoiceDx Skin Scan a medical diagnosis?

No. ChoiceDx Skin Scan is an AI-based skin analysis experience for beauty retail consultation and product recommendation support.
